Intel Behind the Arcade

KGB Arcade is a handcrafted micro-arcade forged in the shadows of the Tactical Underground. Built by KernelGreyBeret (OP ID: TT-KGB), it fuses retro energy, modern neon style, and operator-grade precision into one clean, ad-free universe.

Every game is engineered for asymmetric fun: fast loads, smooth controls, crisp feedback, and that old-school "one more try" loop that hits just right.

The arcade is organized into three vault sections:

8BitOps™ — built from bits, fueled by grit. The regular arcade bangers: retro-flavored neon chaos for the modern operator.
MiniMissions™ — serious fun in small doses. Tactical control-surface games, hidden systems, and objective-driven micro-ops.
TankTots™ — playtime with purpose. Wholesome, kid-friendly learning and big-button fun.

No ads. No trackers. No bloat. Just clean, honest play.
